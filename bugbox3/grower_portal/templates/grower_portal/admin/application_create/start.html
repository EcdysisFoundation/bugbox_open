Application Creation Process

You will complete these 4 steps:

1
Farm & Field Information Farm name, field details, crop types, and sampling date
2
Management Practices Cover crops, amendments, grazing events (if rangeland)
3
Transect Codes & GPS Enter transect codes and map GPS coordinates
4
Field Measurements Drop plate, vegetation, soil, compaction, infiltration data
Linking Applications Later

Applications created without a linked account can be associated with a grower account later through the Django admin interface once the grower creates their account.

Important Notes
  • Choose ONE option: existing account OR manual entry
  • You can save as Draft or mark as Submitted
  • All data will be tracked with your admin account
  • Unlinked applications won't appear in grower dashboards
